Take away

My time here has taught me that many problems can present itself, and the best way to resolve these issues is collaboration. Debugging, analyzing, and monitoring websites goes way beyond the front end code. Analytic teams have to ensure web content is being used efficiently, and hosted servers have to be preforming smoothly to better user experience. Overall there are many assets that make up a marketing team, nothing can be completed by a single being.
